The Marilak Story

Introduction

Marilak Services is a healthcare and public health consulting service provider that offers a range of services catering to the specific needs of the general population and our clients. We offer consulting services for government, health and social care businesses that enable them to improve and streamline their operations .

We also offer s-ervices registration, turnaround services, property sourcing, recruitment services, training services, policies & procedures, DBS services, Ofsted and CQC Registered Managers Readiness Training, and more. Our recruitment services help healthcare facilities find qualified and skilled staff, ensuring that they are providing only the best care for their patients.

Mission

Our mission at Marilak is to make healthcare services accessible and effortless for everyone. We strive to accomplish this by providing top-notch consulting services to health and social care businesses, recruiting and training skilled healthcare staff, and offering supported accommodation services.

Our goal is to simplify your healthcare journey by delivering personalized, compassionate, and reliable care that meets your unique needs. We are committed to improving healthcare outcomes and making a positive impact on the communities we serve.
