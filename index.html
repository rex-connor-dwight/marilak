Our Mission

Our mission at Marilak Services is to improve health outcomes through enhanced capacities and support to service providers and policy makers. We strive to accomplish this through research, promotion of excellent practices and consulting services to health and social care organizations. We also recruit and train skilled healthcare workers, and offer supported accommodation services.
